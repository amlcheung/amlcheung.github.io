~ About Me ~

Hi there! My name is Ashley Cheung and I am currently a candidate for an M.S. in Computer Science at Northeastern University’s Seattle Campus. I am born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area and I moved to Seattle for graduate school. I have really enjoyed living up here, as well as exploring the city and the Pacific Northwest. In 2019, I graduated with a B.S. in Microbiology from Cal Poly, SLO, which is located on California’s beautiful central coast. I have lots of hobbies, which include hiking, skiing, nature photography, horseback riding, wine tasting, trying new restaurants/cafes/bars and exploring small towns. I also love to spend time with my cat, Gus. He is very cute and loves to cuddle!
